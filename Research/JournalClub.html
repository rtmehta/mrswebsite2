Journal Club

Journal Club is a group of students within MRS interested in learning more about new developments in materials engineering. Students volunteer to present a recent scientific article related to the study of materials that they found interesting, with two students presenting each meeting. If you are looking for valuable presentation experience in a supportive peer environment and to expand your knowledge of materials research, Journal Club is for you! No experience is necessary!

MRS Journal Club meets every odd week of the quarter and new members are welcome at anytime!
